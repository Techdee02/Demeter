# Images Required for Demeter Landing Page

Please download and place the following images in this directory:

## 1. hero-maize-field.jpg
**Description:** High-quality photo of a maize/corn field in Kaduna State, Nigeria (or similar Sub-Saharan African setting)
**Specs:** Landscape orientation, 1920x1080px minimum
**Look for:** Golden hour lighting, healthy green maize plants, rural African agricultural setting
**Usage:** Hero section background

## 2. forecast-chart-screenshot.png
**Description:** Screenshot of the 14-day stress forecast chart from the dashboard
**Specs:** 1200x900px, clear visualization
**Look for:** The chart component from /dashboard page showing the ForecastChart with risk predictions
**Usage:** Solution section - dashboard preview
**Note:** You can take this screenshot directly from the dashboard after it loads

## 3. amina-before.jpg
**Description:** Photo depicting struggling/dry maize crops or distressed farmland
**Specs:** 800x600px minimum, aspect ratio 4:3
**Look for:** Wilted crops, dry soil, challenging farming conditions
**Usage:** Amina's story - "Before Demeter" comparison

## 4. amina-after.jpg
**Description:** Photo of healthy, thriving maize crops with lush green growth
**Specs:** 800x600px minimum, aspect ratio 4:3
**Look for:** Vibrant green maize, well-irrigated field, successful harvest
**Usage:** Amina's story - "With Demeter" comparison

## 5. pattern-grid.svg (Optional)
**Description:** Subtle grid pattern for background texture
**Specs:** SVG format, seamless tile
**Look for:** Simple dot grid or line grid pattern
**Usage:** CTA section background pattern
